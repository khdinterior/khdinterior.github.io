About Us

Our Story

KHD INTERIOR was founded in 2015 with a passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces that reflect our clients' personalities and lifestyles. Our team of experienced designers and craftsmen work together to deliver exceptional results.

We believe that everyone deserves to live and work in spaces that inspire them. That's why we offer high-quality interior design services at reasonable prices, making great design accessible to more people.

Our mission is to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments that enhance quality of life and reflect the unique character of each client.
